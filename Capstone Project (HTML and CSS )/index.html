WEM HOTEL

Wem Hotel is an all-day destination for light fare and classic cocktails in a warm, modern setting. Our centrally located bar is the heartbeat of our space where guests, locals, and after work crowd congregate throughout the many parts of the day. During the day we offer light and vibrant offerings from our bar-top cornucopia. By night, the lights dim, and we transition to a buzzing bar scene with approachable cocktails.

OUR STORY

Wem Hotel reflects the true heart of our restaurant through an elevated all day dining experience. Our name is a subtle nod to our central Leda sculpture. Dabbling is the graceful method in which swans submerge their heads under water to eat vegetation.

Start your day with a hearty breakfast buffet, order from the a la cart menu, or grab a coffee and pastries on the go. Lunch features a thoughtful selection of small plates and creative light entrees. Let our barista prepare your favorite beverage as an afternoon treat.

Our centrally located bar is the heartbeat of our space where guests and locals gather throughout the day. At night, the lights dim and Wem Hotel transforms into an evening hot spot with comfort food and tasty cocktails.
